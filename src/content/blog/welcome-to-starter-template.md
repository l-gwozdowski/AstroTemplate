This starter combines a reusable component architecture with sensible defaults for content and layout.

What you get immediately:

1. Accessible header and navigation.
2. Dark and light theme toggle with persistence.
3. Reusable homepage sections.
4. Optional blog routes powered by content collections.

Use it as a baseline and replace copy, styles, and sections to match your brand.
