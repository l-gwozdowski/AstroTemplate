---
title: Build a clear information architecture
description: How to keep components, layouts, and pages easy to scale.
pubDate: 2026-03-20
---

A practical rule:

- `components/layout` for persistent shell pieces.
- `components/sections` for page-level content blocks.
- `layouts` for page wrappers and route scaffolding.
- `pages` for routing and composition only.

This makes large projects easier to navigate and refactor.
