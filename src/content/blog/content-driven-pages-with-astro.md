Astro content collections let you validate frontmatter and generate pages safely.

In this starter, the `blog` collection supports:

- `title`
- `description`
- `pubDate`
- `updatedDate` (optional)
- `draft` (optional)

This approach scales well from small blogs to larger knowledge bases.
